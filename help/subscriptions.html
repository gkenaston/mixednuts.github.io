Subscriptions

Plans, pricing, and managing your subscription.

Free Plan

The Free plan includes everything you need to organize a single group:

  • 1 group
  • Unlimited members
  • Recurring schedules and roster management
  • Push notifications and email notifications
  • Group chat
  • Manual slot management

Pro Plan

The Pro plan is for organizers who run multiple groups or want automated roster management:

  • Everything in the Free plan
  • Up to 10 groups
  • AutoAssign -- reserves are automatically promoted in FIFO order with confirmation timeouts
  • RoundRobin -- leader designation rotates across matches

Pricing

  • Monthly: $5 per month
  • Annual: $50 per year (save 17%)

Both plans auto-renew until canceled.

Upgrading to Pro

To upgrade, go to Profile > Upgrade to Pro. You will be taken to a secure Stripe checkout page to enter your payment details and select your billing period (monthly or annual). Once payment is complete, Pro features are available immediately.

Managing Your Subscription

To manage your subscription (update payment method, switch between monthly and annual, or view invoices), go to Profile > Manage Subscription. This opens the Stripe customer portal where you can make changes directly.

Canceling

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time through the Stripe customer portal (Profile > Manage Subscription). When you cancel:

  • Your Pro features remain active until the end of your current billing period.
  • After the billing period ends, your account reverts to the Free plan.
  • If you have more than 1 group, you will need to leave or delete groups until you are at 1 group to continue using the Free plan.
  • No refunds are provided for partial billing periods.

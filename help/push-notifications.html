Help / Push Notifications

Push Notifications

Stay informed about roster changes, promotions, and chat messages.

Android

On Android, push notifications are enabled by default when you install Mixed Nuts. You can manage notification settings in your device's system settings under Apps > Mixed Nuts > Notifications.

iOS

On iOS, Mixed Nuts will ask for notification permission the first time you open the app. If you denied the prompt, you can enable notifications later by going to Settings > Notifications > Mixed Nuts and toggling "Allow Notifications" on.

In-App Toggle

Mixed Nuts includes an in-app push notification toggle under Profile > Push Notifications. This controls whether the app registers your device for push notifications. If disabled, you will not receive any push notifications from Mixed Nuts regardless of your system settings.

What Triggers Notifications

You will receive push notifications for:

  • Match reminders -- upcoming match notifications
  • Roster changes -- when someone opts in or out of a match you are in
  • Reserve promotion -- when you are promoted from reserve to confirmed
  • Slot offers -- when you are offered a slot and need to confirm (AutoAssign/RoundRobin)
  • Chat messages -- new messages in your group chats (unless muted)
  • Thread replies -- when someone replies to a message thread you started
  • Reactions -- when someone reacts to one of your messages
  • Match cancellations and edits -- when a match you opted into is canceled or rescheduled
  • Removed from group or match -- when an admin removes you
  • Invite accepted -- when a new member joins a group you own or admin

Notification Inbox

Tap the bell icon on the Home tab to open the notification inbox — a complete history of every notification you have received, including ones you already dismissed. A dot on the bell means you have unread notifications. Tap any row to mark it read and jump to the related match, thread, or chat. Use the toolbar action to mark everything read at once.

Chat messages and match reminders are intentionally excluded from the inbox. Chat messages live in the chat tab; match reminders live in the schedule. See the notification inbox help page for the full list of what appears.

Troubleshooting

If you are not receiving notifications:

  1. Check system settings -- make sure notifications are enabled for Mixed Nuts in your device's notification settings.
  2. Check the in-app toggle -- go to Profile > Push Notifications and make sure it is enabled.
  3. Check your internet connection -- push notifications require an active internet connection to be delivered.
  4. Restart the app -- close and reopen Mixed Nuts to re-register your device token.
  5. Check Do Not Disturb -- make sure your device is not in Do Not Disturb or Focus mode.

If notifications still are not working after trying these steps, contact us through the app.

Delete Your Account

You can delete your Mixed Nuts account and all associated data at any time.

From the App

  1. Open Mixed Nuts
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account and profile information
  • Your group memberships
  • Your match participation history
  • Your chat messages
  • Your push notification registrations

Groups you own will also be deleted, including all associated matches, members, and chat history.

Subscription

If you have an active Pro subscription, cancel it through your device's subscription settings (App Store or Google Play) before deleting your account. Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription.

Timeframe

Account deletion is immediate. This action cannot be undone.
